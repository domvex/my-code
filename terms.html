Worker Terms & Service Policies

1. 10-Day Service Warranty

Domvex provides a 10-day warranty on all completed services. If a service issue recurs within this period, the assigned worker is obligated to revisit and resolve the issue free of cost. Domvex will not charge the customer, and no additional payment will be processed for this re-visit.

2. Anti-Poaching & Solicitation Policy

Worker poaching is strictly prohibited. Providing your personal contact details or business cards to customers, or soliciting them for direct work outside of the Domvex platform, is a severe violation of our agreement. If caught, your account will be permanently banned, and strict legal action may be taken.

3. Performance-Based Commission

Our commission structure is dynamic and based on your performance. Maintaining a high customer rating is essential. Workers with consistently high ratings are eligible for lower commission slabs, allowing you to increase your earnings on every job.

4. Material Selection & Liability

If you are providing materials for a service, you must offer three options to the customer: Premium, Standard, and Economical. You are required to explain the quality and cost differences to the customer and obtain their approval before using any material. If any appliance is damaged or the service fails due to the use of inferior or unauthorized materials without customer consent, the full liability for the damage will rest solely on you, and the cost will be recovered from your earnings.
