About Domvex

Redefining on-demand home services across the nation with trust, transparency, and efficiency.

Our Story

Domvex was founded with a singular vision: to revolutionize the highly unorganized home services industry in India. Finding reliable, background-verified, and skilled professionals for everyday essential tasks has always been a major hassle for households. Domvex was born to solve this exact problem.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, secure, and hassle-free booking platform that connects households with 100% verified, heavily trained, and professional technicians at fair and standardized pricing.
